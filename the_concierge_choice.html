The Concierge Choice

The Concierge Choice allows Dr. Johnson to spend much more time with you, managing all your health concerns in a convenient and comprehensive manner. It’s the return of good old-fashioned medicine, where you had a physician who knew you and had time to discuss all of your medical concerns, provide support and help you when you needed it most.

Program Benefits

More Time with Dr. Johnson

There is no substitute for a doctor that knows you. As a concierge physician, Dr. Johnson can take the extra time necessary to understand your health history and challenges, consult with you, provide you with care and treatment in a comprehensive and compassionate manner.


Preventive Healthcare

An extended annual examination that goes beyond what insurance or Medicare would cover, including a thorough health review and supplementary testing customized to your unique needs.


Easy Ways to Reach Dr. Johnson Directly

All concierge patients receive Dr. Johnson’s private cell phone number so that they can reach him if they have a health concern after hours. You will also receive Dr. Johnson’s private email address so you can correspond conveniently for non-urgent matters.


Patient Advocacy

What happens to you outside of the office is very important to Dr. Johnson. He can serve as your advocate with other health providers and specialists, helping you make important medical decisions in the context of your whole health history. Dr. Johnson can even coordinate your care should you be admitted to the hospital.


Relaxed Appointments

Your care is provided in a comforting environment conducive to good health. Appointments are scheduled for longer, and you won’t have excessive waiting room delays.


Convenience

In most cases, you will be seen the same day or next day you call, and appointments will be scheduled with convenience in mind.


Enhanced Coordination of Referrals

The office can assist you with scheduling appointments with specialists when necessary.
