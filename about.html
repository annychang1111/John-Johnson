About Dr. Johnson

Dr. Johnson began serving the people of Batesville, Indiana in 1998. Before that, he practiced in the small town of Beavercreek, Ohio after completing his residency in Internal Medicine as Chief Resident. His goal has always been to become familiar with his patient’s unique situation and tailor treatment personally. This level of care cannot be adequately provided in the typical 5 minute office visit.
